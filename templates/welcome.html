The Official Rules of Rock, Paper, Scissors..

There are five simple rules to Rock, Paper, Scissors..

  • First rule, you do not talk about RPS..

  • Second rule, you DO NOT talk about RPS ..

  • Rock crushes scissors..

How To Play..

So, Rock beats Scissors, Scissors beats Paper, Paper beats Rock..

Players take turns choosing one of the three, to decide who wins..

Should players make the same choice this results in a tie..
